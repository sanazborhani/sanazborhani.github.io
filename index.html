About

I am a licensed Professional Engineer with a PhD in Water Resources Engineering and more than a decade of experience delivering hydrologic and hydraulic analyses for bridges, floodplains, and climate resilience projects. I work extensively with HEC‑RAS, SRH‑2D, HEC‑HMS, SWMM, and ArcGIS, and I build Python tools to automate watershed and modeling workflows.
